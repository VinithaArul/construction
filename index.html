ABOUT US

Vicky Construction Pvt Ltd. was founded in 1995 under the stewardship of Mr.B S VIGNESH , a qualified Civil Engineer. Since its inception, Akshaya has set higher standards for itself, inspired by the Sanskrit origin of its name, which means ‘endless pursuit’. It has grown into one of the most respected real estate companies in India, reputed for its transparent business practices and innovation.
